
# 🛍️ Consumer Shopping Behavior Analysis  
### Leveraging Data to Drive Business Strategy

---

## 📌 Business Problem

A leading retail company aims to better understand customer shopping behavior to improve:

- 📈 Sales performance  
- 😊 Customer satisfaction  
- 🔁 Long-term customer loyalty  

Management has observed changing purchasing patterns across:

- Demographics  
- Product categories  
- Online vs Offline channels  
- Seasonal variations  
- Discounts and promotional campaigns  

The company wants to answer:

> **How can consumer shopping data be leveraged to identify trends, improve engagement, and optimize marketing and product strategies?**

---

## 🎯 Project Objective

This project performs a comprehensive data analysis to:

- Identify purchasing trends
- Analyze impact of discounts and reviews
- Study seasonal buying behavior
- Understand payment preferences
- Detect drivers of repeat purchases
- Generate actionable business insights

---

## 🛠️ Tech Stack

| Tool | Purpose |
|------|----------|
| **Python** | Data cleaning, preprocessing, EDA |
| **SQL** | Data extraction, transformation, aggregations |
| **Power BI** | Interactive dashboards & business visualization |

---

## 📂 Project Workflow

### 1️⃣ Data Extraction (SQL)

- Query structured consumer dataset
- Aggregate sales by category, demographics, and channel
- Compute KPIs like:
  - Total Revenue
  - Average Order Value
  - Repeat Purchase Rate
  - Seasonal Sales Trends

---

### 2️⃣ Data Processing & Analysis (Python)

- Handled missing values
- Feature engineering
- Exploratory Data Analysis (EDA)
- Trend visualization
- Correlation analysis

Key analysis included:

- Discount vs Purchase behavior
- Review rating impact on buying decision
- Online vs Offline revenue comparison
- Seasonal demand shifts
- Payment method distribution

---

### 3️⃣ Business Intelligence Dashboard (Power BI)

Created interactive dashboards to visualize:

- 📊 Revenue trends over time
- 🛒 Category-wise performance
- 🔁 Repeat vs One-time customers
- 📦 Sales channel distribution

---

## 📈 Key Insights

- Certain demographics show higher repeat purchase rates.
- Discount-driven sales spike during seasonal events.
- Online channels outperform offline in specific categories.
- Positive reviews strongly correlate with higher conversion rates.
- Payment preference impacts purchase completion rates.
